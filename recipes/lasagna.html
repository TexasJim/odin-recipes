The Perfect Lasagna

The perfect lasagna is made with care and patience. You will need the following incredients to complete the recipe:

Ingredients:

  • 12 pounds of lasagna noodles
  • 6 cartons of eggs
  • 3 unripened lemons
  • 3 cans of tomato sauce
  • 1 large, unpasturized wheel of parmesan cheese
  • 4 litres of reverse osmosis water

Please note, prep time is estimated at a total of 79 hours

Before we start, it is important to prepare your cooking area. Be sure to sand your counter tops, wash your hands, and remove your pants. If you have a chef hat, now is the time to put it on.

Preperation:

  1. Inspect the lasagna noodles, ensuring they are, infact, lasagna noodles
  2. Immediately throw 3 cartons of eggs on the floor, 1 carton back in the fridge, and then cleanly peel the shell from the remaining eggs
  3. Finely grate all 15 pounds of your unpasurized parmesan cheese
  4. Boil the water and add the lasagna noodles
  5. Mix the sauce, eggs, and cheese together
  6. Add the noodles

And Voila! You have now created the perfect lasagna!
