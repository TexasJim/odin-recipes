Hamburger

The Best Hamburger You Ever Had in Your Life

Making yourself a burger requires a certain amount of self loathing and overall dissatisfaction with life. Make sure to feel these emotions deeply before beginning.

Ingredients:

  • 7kg lean ground beef
  • 14 white onions
  • 1/2 cup pink salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon organic ketchup
  • 6oz goose liver
  • 2 cups raw, unfiltered malt vinegar
  • 1 whole ass bag of store bought hamburger buns

Please note, prep time should take no more than a total of 4 minutes. If you hit 5 minutes at any point, you have ruined the whole project and must start again. Thank you.

Preperation:

  1. Preheat barbeque or oven
  2. Combine all ingredients in an oppropriately sized bowl
  3. Shape the ingredient combination into small triangles
  4. Grill em up on the BBQ!
  5. Once cooked to desired temperature, remove from head
  6. Prepare the buns with desired condiments
  7. Slap the meat down on your bun
  8. Take a brief moment to look in the mirror and reflect on your life
  9. Wonder to yourself, "how did I get here?"
  10. "What exactly did I do to deserve this?"
  11. And then take a big 'ol bite of your burger!
  12. Try to hold back tears until you've finished, so you don't end up with a soggy bun
