Eggs: Plain and Simple

Making yourself eggs is probably one of the most basic tasks in life. Failing to make eggs correctly (for either yourself or someone else) may result in immediate extermination from the planet.

Think carefully before beginning.

Ingredients:

  • Eggs
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Preperation

  1. Preheat the pan
  2. Smash the eggs directly in the pan
  3. Sprinkle in salt and pepper to taste
  4. All finished, you can now enjoy your eggs
